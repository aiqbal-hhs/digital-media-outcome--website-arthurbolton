Synth Plugins


Sound design is an essential part of music these days and so it's important to have good quality sythesizers at your disposal. While default synths in many DAWs are fine, some just lack the capability or flexibility to do certain things and some are just hard to use. All the synths I will talk about here are synths that bring something unique to the table or are just really powerful.

Matt Tytel - Vital

Many people say that Vital is like free Serum because it's so high quality. This is absolutely true. Of all the plugins I talk about across all pages of this website, Vital is easily the best. It's a wavetable synth with has enough features to create virtually any sound you can think of. It has many features designed to edit waveforms and you can get drastically different sounds even from the most basic of waveforms. It has easy modulation and custom LFO shapes which you can drag to automate any parameter you can think of. Above all of that, it has a seperate window to add effects to the sound such as distortion, chorus and delay. These effects are simple to use and are quite high quality. The main thing I love about the plugin is that all of the changes being made to the sound are represented visually whith animations which makes the plugin really easy to learn and is a great introduction to sound design. Overall, you absolutely need this plugin.

U-HE - Podolski

Podolski is a synthesizer made by U-HE. The main feature of the synth is it's build it arpeggiator section which can be used to make great sounding patterns. On the main synthesis page, the synth contains one oscillator, a filter, 2 effects (chorus and delay), two LFOs and an envelope. The synth is also polyphonic with up to 16 voices, however you can also set it to be mono, legato and arpeggiated too. The arpeggiator tab gives you controls over the individual note length, the pitch of each note, how fast the notes are played and many other useful controls. This section can create amazing sounding arpeggios which are relatively easy to understand. If sound designing things yourself isn't really your thing though, the synth comes with over 300 great sounding presets to get you started. Overall, this synth is an amazing tool to have at your disposal and is an easy way to start making arpeggios.
