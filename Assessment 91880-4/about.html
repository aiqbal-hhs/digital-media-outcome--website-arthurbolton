About


I'm Aardvark04 and I'm a music producer from New Zealand. I made this website as part of a school project but I also made it to document some of my favourite free plugins. The reason I think this is important is because the quality of the default plugins in my DAW (FL Studio) aren't always the greatest and many of the interfaces are confusing. I think that these plugins really do help with the music production process and I hope you think so too.
